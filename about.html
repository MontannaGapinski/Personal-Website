About Me

I am Montanna Gapinski, a first-year student studying Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am from central Minnesota and was drawn to Colorado for its strong presence in the Engineering world!

My interest in Computer Science started from a young age when my love for video games drove me to build my own PC. From there, I embraced my problem-solving skills and hobby of playing online games and chose to apply them using CS.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling to new places and trying things I have not done before, playing video games, reading, and volunteering at the local humane society.
